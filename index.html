OUR COURSES

Sea Life

Hands-on Marine Biology Intermediate

Take diving lessons with some of our experts and follow along on our field work with these sea creatures. We will be observing the sea life in their natural habitat.

Tropics of Asia

Field Trip through Ancient Greenery Beginner

A trip through the Indias and South-east Asias and through its rich history! Join our historians and learn about both manmade wonders such as the Angkor Wat and the wildlife in their homes.

Polar Expedition

Witness the Arctic & Alaska Advanced

A summer expedition to the freezing (now melting) points of the earth. Follow along with geologists as we investigate both global warming effects on the icy regions while also hiking up to the snowcapped mountains.

FAQs

1 it too dangerous for teens?

There are age limits that suit each expedition. We ensure that our itinerary is safe and away from any possible dangers. Teens, however, are required to act on their own behalf with responsibility in following closely with the experts' instructions.

2What can teens benefit from this?

Here at Observanture, we believe that lifelong learners don't remain confined to ordinary classroom environments. By working within the field, teens will see both how the professional and natural world works together in their learning journey.

3Can parents come along?

Parents can come along on their own independent travels. However, teens are recommended to learn how to work on their own to get as much experience. No provisions such as protective clothing and equipment would be provided to parents who come along.
